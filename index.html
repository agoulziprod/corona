About me

Hi!
I'm Mohamed, I found the idea when I was meeting people, and although I know that you have to stay distant, I was the first one to shake hands, because, I don't know how to explain it, but it's a habit that we've grown up with! It's difficult to change it! And even if we want to change it, it has to be a powerful reason! and we as Moroccans! we considered shaking hands like a sign of love, so we have to change the concept that, although you don't shake hands means you still like him/her! then I said why don't I make these stickers and share them with people! so everyone could print them, stick them to the Pc, wall, anything else! Like that, we all are going to change the way we look at things, and salaaam!
